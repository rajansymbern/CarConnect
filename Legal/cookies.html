1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how CarConnect ("we," "our," or "us") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our car leasing platform. This policy should be read alongside our Privacy Policy.

By using our platform, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy. You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as your preferred language and other settings, which can make your next visit easier and the site more useful to you.

Cookies can be "persistent" or "session" cookies. Persistent cookies remain on your device after you close your browser, while session cookies are deleted when you close your browser.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the platform to function properly and cannot be disabled. They include:

  • Authentication cookies that keep you logged in
  • Security cookies that protect against fraud
  • Session cookies that maintain your current session
  • Load balancing cookies that distribute traffic

Functional Cookies

These cookies enhance your experience by remembering your preferences:

  • Language and region preferences
  • User interface customization
  • Form data that you've entered
  • Search history and filters

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our platform:

  • Page views and navigation patterns
  • Time spent on different sections
  • Error tracking and performance monitoring
  • User journey analysis

Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver relevant advertisements:

  • Ad targeting and personalization
  • Campaign effectiveness measurement
  • Retargeting and remarketing
  • Social media integration

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that place cookies on your device:

  • Google Analytics: For website analytics and performance monitoring
  • Calendly: For scheduling consultation appointments
  • Payment Processors: For secure payment processing
  • Social Media Platforms: For social sharing and integration
  • Customer Support Tools: For providing customer assistance

These third-party services have their own privacy policies and cookie practices. We encourage you to review their policies.

5. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To keep you logged in and secure your account
  • Preferences: To remember your settings and preferences
  • Security: To protect against fraud and unauthorized access
  • Performance: To monitor and improve platform performance
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with our platform
  • Personalization: To provide relevant content and features
  • Marketing: To deliver targeted advertisements and promotions

6. Cookie Duration

Cookies on our platform have different lifespans:

  • Session Cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain for a set period (typically 30 days to 2 years)
  • Authentication Cookies: May last for extended periods for convenience
  • Analytics Cookies: Usually expire after 1-2 years

7. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have several options for managing cookies:

Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings:

  • Block all cookies
  • Block third-party cookies only
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Set preferences for specific websites

Cookie Consent

When you first visit our platform, you'll see a cookie consent banner that allows you to:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • Customize your cookie preferences
  • Learn more about our cookie usage

Opt-Out Tools

You can also use opt-out tools for specific types of cookies:

  • Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on
  • Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out tool
  • Network Advertising Initiative opt-out tool

8. Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you choose to disable cookies, please be aware that:

  • Some platform features may not function properly
  • You may need to log in repeatedly
  • Your preferences may not be saved
  • Some content may not be personalized
  • Security features may be limited

Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they are necessary for basic platform functionality.
