Accessibility Statement

Last updated: July 2025

1. Our Commitment to Accessibility

CarConnect is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

We believe that technology should be accessible to all users, regardless of their abilities or disabilities. Our goal is to provide an inclusive experience that allows everyone to benefit from our car leasing platform.

2. Standards Compliance

We strive to conform to the following accessibility standards:

  • WCAG 2.1: Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (Level AA)
  • ADA Title III: Americans with Disabilities Act requirements
  • Section 508: Rehabilitation Act accessibility standards
  • EN 301 549: European accessibility standards

While we aim for full compliance, we acknowledge that some areas may still need improvement. We are actively working to address any accessibility issues.

3. Accessibility Features

Our platform includes the following accessibility features:

Visual Accessibility

  • High contrast color schemes and text
  • Resizable text that can be enlarged up to 200%
  • Clear typography with good readability
  • Consistent visual hierarchy and layout
  • Alternative text for images and graphics

Keyboard Navigation

  • Full keyboard accessibility for all functions
  • Visible focus indicators for navigation
  • Logical tab order throughout the platform
  • Keyboard shortcuts for common actions
  • Skip navigation links for main content

Screen Reader Support

  • Semantic HTML structure for proper interpretation
  • ARIA labels and landmarks for navigation
  • Descriptive link text and button labels
  • Form labels and error messages
  • Table headers and data relationships

Audio and Video

  • Captions for video content
  • Audio descriptions where necessary
  • Transcripts for audio content
  • Volume controls and pause functionality

4. Mobile Accessibility

Our mobile experience includes accessibility features:

  • Touch targets that meet minimum size requirements (44px)
  • Gesture alternatives for complex interactions
  • Responsive design that works across devices
  • Voice control compatibility
  • Screen reader optimization for mobile
  • Safe area support for different screen sizes

5. Known Limitations

While we strive for full accessibility, we acknowledge some current limitations:

  • Some third-party integrations may not be fully accessible
  • Complex data visualizations may need alternative formats
  • Certain interactive elements may require additional testing
  • PDF documents may not be fully accessible

We are actively working to address these limitations and improve accessibility across all features.

6. Testing and Evaluation

We regularly test our platform for accessibility using:

  • Automated accessibility testing tools
  • Manual testing with assistive technologies
  • User testing with people who have disabilities
  • Expert accessibility audits and reviews
  • Continuous monitoring and feedback collection

Our accessibility testing includes screen readers, keyboard navigation, color contrast analysis, and other assistive technologies.

7. Feedback and Support

We welcome feedback on accessibility issues and suggestions for improvement:

  • Report accessibility issues you encounter
  • Suggest improvements for better accessibility
  • Request alternative formats for content
  • Provide feedback on assistive technology compatibility

We are committed to responding to accessibility feedback within 5 business days and addressing issues promptly.

8. Alternative Access Methods

If you experience difficulties accessing our platform, we offer alternative methods:

  • Phone Support: Call us for assistance with platform access
  • Email Support: Contact us for alternative content formats
  • Video Calls: Schedule a consultation with screen sharing
  • Documentation: Request accessible versions of documents
  • Training: Receive personalized platform training

9. Continuous Improvement

We are committed to ongoing accessibility improvement:

  • Regular accessibility audits and assessments
  • Staff training on accessibility best practices
  • Integration of accessibility into development processes
  • Monitoring of new accessibility standards and guidelines
  • User feedback integration into design decisions

We review and update this accessibility statement regularly to reflect our current practices and improvements.
