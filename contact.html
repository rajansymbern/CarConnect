Frequently Asked Questions

How does CarConnect work?

CarConnect connects trusted networks for car leasing. Car owners can lease their vehicles to family and friends, earning extra income while helping their network.

Is CarConnect available in my area?

We're currently in development and will be launching in select areas. Join our waitlist to be notified when we're available in your region.

What are the insurance requirements?

All vehicles must have valid insurance coverage. We provide guidance on insurance requirements and may offer additional coverage options.

How do payments work?

Payments are processed securely through our platform. Lessees pay in advance, and lessors receive payments monthly with transparent fee structures.
