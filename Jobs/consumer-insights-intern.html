Introduction

Ready to shape the future of a major life milestone?

Remember the feeling of getting your first car? That wild mix of freedom, excitement, and maybe a little bit of anxiety? At CarConnect for Family, we're on a mission to make that journey smoother, safer, and more collaborative for young adults and their parents.

We're an exciting startup looking for creative, driven, and passionate interns to help us build the future of car sharing. This isn't a coffee-fetching internship. You will be a core part of the team, and your voice will directly shape our product, our brand, and our growth.

Your Mission: Be the Voice of Our User

You are the detective, the empath, the truth-seeker. Your mission is to understand the real thoughts, feelings, and pain points of families navigating the first-car process. You'll ensure we build a product that people actually need and love.
